In this question we want to identify any animals that have died. They may or may not have died as a result of drought or resulting diseases; however if there are a lot of dead animals in a particular area this would be of interest. Significant numbers of dead livestock can have a negative effect on their owner's livelihood, and a flow-on negative effect on the local economy.

Aside from the loss of livelihood associated with the loss of animals, carcases of animals lying in the streets or near water bodies can also be a threat to public health.
