This is a simple filtering question to remove any photos that do not have domestic animals in them and therefore are not relevant to the analysis.

Click "Yes" if you see a domestic animal or animals in the photo. Some of the animals you are likely to see in these photos include cows, cats, dogs, horses, elephants, buffalo, sheep, goats, pigs, chickens, and any other livestock.

You should also click "Yes" if the animal is dead and/or if you are looking at a skeleton.
